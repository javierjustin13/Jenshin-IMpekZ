Conquer The World of Jenshin IMpekZ
Gameplay
Explore The Vast World of Teyvat
Teyvat is a vast and fantastical world that serves as the setting for the popular open-world action RPG Jenshin IMpekZ. The world of Teyvat is divided into seven regions, each with its own unique climate, culture, and people. Explore the never ending mystery of Teyvat
Build Your Character Dream Team
With over 30 unique characters to choose from, each with their own set of elemental abilities, talents, and strengths, you can make the perfect dream team that you want. Your character dream team is the key of completing this game.
Battle The Fierce Bosses of Teyvat
These bosses are challenging enemies that require careful planning and execution to defeat. Each boss has unique abilities and weaknesses, which players must learn to exploit to emerge victorious. Will you be able to defeat the formidable bosses of Teyvat?
Collect Artifacts to Enchance Your Character
Artifacts are powerful pieces of equipment that enhance a character's abilities and can significantly impact their performance in combat. These artifacts can be collected by exploring the game world, completing quests, or defeating bosses. Boost your character attributes using artifacts
Enter Domain to Co-op with Other Players
In Jenshin IMpekZ, players have the opportunity to team up with others to tackle challenging domains in co-op mode. Domains are special locations that offer valuable rewards and are filled with powerful enemies that require careful strategy and teamwork to defeat.
